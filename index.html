ホワイトアウトサバイバルの「熊狩り」とは

熊狩りは、同盟のメンバーが協力して巨大な熊に「集結」し、与えたダメージの合計を競う定期イベントです。最終的なダメージは、どの英雄を集結主（左）に置くか・兵種の比率・専用装備・天賦などの組み合わせで大きく変わります。「強い英雄を入れたのに伸びない」「同じ戦力なのに人によって差が出る」といったことが起きやすく、正しい知識があるかどうかで結果が何倍も変わるのが熊狩りの面白さであり難しさです。当サイトのツールは、この「変わりやすさ」を試算・可視化して、ムダな課金や強化を避けながら効率よく伸ばすことを目的にしています。

About "Whiteout Tools Lab"

This site is a free set of strategy tools and guides for Japanese-speaking — and now English-speaking — players of the mobile strategy game Whiteout Survival. Our theme is beating alliance events like Bear Hunt and PvP (Foundry Battle, Castle Battle) by the numbers rather than by feel. Everything is made and maintained by hand by an active player (Jasmine), based on in-game testing and public information.

What is Bear Hunt in Whiteout Survival?

Bear Hunt is a recurring event where alliance members rally a giant bear together and compete on total damage. Final damage shifts hugely with who you set as rally leader (left), your troop ratio, exclusive gear and talents. It's easy to see "I added a strong hero but it didn't help" or big gaps between players with similar power — which is exactly why the right knowledge can multiply your result. Our tools estimate and visualize that variability so you can grow efficiently and avoid wasted spending or upgrades.
